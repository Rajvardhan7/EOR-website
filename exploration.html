Exploration Services

Exploration Services:

  • Integrated Analysis and interpretation of G&G data
    • Study of available seismic, geological and petrophysical data.
    • Identifying the sequence boundaries and correlatable seismic markers in 2D/3D for mapping.
    • Well log interpretation of available wells and correlation of various formations and boundaries.
    • Chronostratigraphic correlation of identified surfaces using 2D/3D seismic data and preparation of different maps namely Isochron, Isopach, Time and Depth maps.
    • Seismic attribute analysis for selected zones of interests and reservoir characterisation.
    • Identification of leads and prospects.
  • Basin analysis and study of petroleum system
    • Using integrated G&G data interpretation, identification of possible petroleum system at basin scale.
    • Identification of possible play system through study of geological factors like Source rock, Reservoir rock and Cap rock.
    • Linking the identified prospects with known and/or new play.
  • Resource Assessment and risk analysis
    • Estimation of STIOIP for all the prospects identified in the study area under 1P (Proved), 2P (Proved+Probable) and 3P ( Proved+Probable+Possible) category using SPE classification.
    • Risk analysis for chance of success using Risk model for each element of petroleum system to arrive at risked resource volume.
    • Assessment of Recoverable Volume based on Reservoir parameters and Productivity Performance including assessment of drive mechanism and expected fluid characteristics.
  • Evaluation of Exploration license blocks
    • Technical and commercial evaluation of blocks offered for exploration under bidding roundsas per terms and conditions of the NIO (Notice inviting Offers).
    • Ranking of blocks based on prospectivity evaluationand both Surface and Subsurface Risk Analysis.
    • Formulation of Exploration plan to be included in bid for successful bidding.
