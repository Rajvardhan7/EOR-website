Ajay Kumar Dwivedi

Former Director (Exploration)-ONGC having a distinguished career of more than 38 years in ONGC. While on the Board of ONGC, he had additional responsibilities of Director Incharge-HSE, Director Incharge-MM and Director Incharge-Carbon management and Sustainability group of ONGC. He was also Chairman of the joint venture company ONGC-TERI Biotechnology Ltd.(OTBL).

Played key role in completing two projects initiated by Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MOPNG) namely Resource Reassessment of Indian Sedimentary Basins and National Seismic Program for unapprised Basins of India.

As an outcome of the exploration strategy formulated during his tenure as Director (Exploration), ONGC, two new Basins namely Bengal Basin and Vindhyan Basin came into advanced stage of exploration through commercial discoveries made. He also got the Petroleum Resource Management System (PRMS) implemented in ONGC for Hydrocarbon Reseves Estimation. Costant focus on exploration also resulted in making commercial discoveries in Kutch offshore Basin from Mesozoic play and in the area West of Mumbai High field, both on the west coast of India. Development plans were formulated and approved for these discoveries through his active involvement.

Worked in various Indian Sedimentary Basins and institutes (Western Offshore and Onshore, Cauvery, Assam and Assam Arakan Fold Belt and Mahanadi, Bengal and Andaman Basins and Keshav Dev Malviya Institute of Petroleum Exploration (KDMIPE).

He was a Core Team member of Joint Project team on Organizational Change Mangement Program, involving redesign of structure, systems and business processes of ONGC. He was also Chairman of Steering Committee entrusted with the job of project on Petroleum Systems and Sequence Stratigraphy of sedimentary basins of India.

He has undergone various Executive Development Programs including those at Indian School of Business (ISB), Hyderabad and School of Business, University of Alberta, Canada.

Post superannuation from ONGC, he was engaged as Advisor to ONGC with a specific task of Redefining the role of R&D institutes to address current and emerging technological challenges. He also holds the responsibility as Member of Research Council of CSIR-NGRI.
