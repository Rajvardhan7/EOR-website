    • Well Performance Analysis, Diagnosis and production Optimisation.

  • Water Flood management / surveillance
    • Pattern Balancing - Allocation of production / injection based on VRR/ IVRR.
    • Estimation of pattern recovery and overall field recovery.
    • Identification of offending layers &interconnectivity between producers and injectors and recompletion of wells for production optimisation based on Tracer survey / interference test etc.
    • Performance analysis and optimisation of A/L system.
    • Rigorous monitoring of water injection quality and quantity both.
    • Dykstra parsons coefficient for estimation of vertical heterogeneity and estimation of sweep efficiency of a water flood process.
