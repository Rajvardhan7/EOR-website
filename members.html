Sidhartha Sur

A petroleum engineer with ~38 years of experience in the different areas of Reservoir Engineering with particular emphasis in Enhanced Oil Recovery processes. Strong experience in Screening, Laboratory evaluation, Pilot design and field implementation/testing and trouble shooting of EOR (For development of Light, Medium and Heavy Oil fields) through application of Steam, Air, HC, CO2, Polymer, ASP processes. Grew up with Air injection EOR process of ONGC in Mehsana involving Laboratory evaluation, Pilot design, Implementation, Evaluation of pilot, and Commercialisation of process. Developed Expert System (“AskEOR”) based on environment, lithology, rock & fluid properties, reservoir & production characteristics for Screening & Ranking different EOR processes. Used it for Colombian fields in Dec 2019 as part of services rendered to M/S Consultec.
