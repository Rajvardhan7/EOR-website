Sidhartha Sur

A petroleum engineer with ~38 years of experience in the different areas of Reservoir Engineering with particular emphasis in Enhanced Oil Recovery processes. Strong experience in Screening, Laboratory evaluation, Pilot design and field implementation/testing and trouble shooting of EOR (For development of Light, Medium and Heavy Oil fields) through application of Steam, Air, HC, CO2, Polymer, ASP processes. Grew up with Air injection EOR process of ONGC in Mehsana involving Laboratory evaluation, Pilot design, Implementation, Evaluation of pilot, and Commercialisation of process. Developed Expert System (“AskEOR”) based on environment, lithology, rock & fluid properties, reservoir & production characteristics for Screening & Ranking different EOR processes. Used it for Colombian fields in Dec 2019 as part of services rendered to M/S Consultec.

Served as Advisor to ONGC and its Group of Companies in the fields of EOR and IOR processes in 2018-19. Before superannuation in ONGC on 1st Feb 2018, was heading Institute of Reservoir Studies (IRS), a premier institute of ONGC (National Oil Company), India during 2016-18. All investment decision of ONGC (Development of Green fields/Re-Development of Brown fields through IOR/EOR efforts of ONGC) and JV fields are based on the results of IRS studies. Earlier was Chief of Corporate planning of ONGC during 2014-16.

Served as CTO during 2010 and as CEO during 2010-2012 in Mansarovar Energy Colombia Ltd, a multi-cultural joint venture between Ecopetrol, Colombia, ONGC Videsh Ltd, India and SINOPEC, China. Synergistic approach introduced with emphasis on scientific teamwork thereby mitigating sub-surface and surface risks. Outcome was increase in heavy oil (2000 cP-10,000cP) production from 25K bopd to 40K bopd through cold & hot production in 3 years time with improved financial & HSE performance from Jazmin, Girasol, Moriche and Abarco fields of Magdalena Basin, Colombia. For the growth of the company, led the team while participating in the Colombian bidding rounds and winning an exploratory block in Llanos Basin.

Proficient in developing & implementing exploitation (IOR & EOR) technologies for volatile oil, light, medium and heavy oil in sandstone & carbonate reservoirs.

Dealt with “Reservoir Management” of Oil & Gas reservoirs operating under Water drive, Modified water, Gas and Air drive. Management of dry and rich gas reservoirs under depletion & water drive for all fields of ONGC.

Currently involved in EOR operations in a field in South America.

Presented and published technical papers in SPE and in different national & international forums.

Imparted classroom training to personnel of OIL, India and STAATSOLIE, Suriname in 2003-04 on Thermal Recovery Methods. 5 days Classroom training on “EOR : What you need to know” to 5 batches of reservoir engineers of ONGC at IIT , Dhanbad during 2017-19.

Provided internship to about 30 pre-final year petroleum engineering students of MIT, Pune, UPES, Dehradun and IIT (ISM) Dhanbad during May-July 2020.

Currently Adjunct Professor in IIT (ISM) Dhanbad in the Deptt of Petroleum Engineering. Served as SPE Distinguished Lecturer in 2009-10, SPE Regional Latin American & Caribbean awardee on completion and Technology in 2012 and SPE Distinguished Member in 2017, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad Distinguished Alumnus awardee of 2016.
